Privacy Policy

Your privacy and trust are paramount to us. Learn how we safeguard your information.

1. Introduction

At Kodai Blossom Home Stay, we are committed to protecting the personal data of our guests. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information that you provide to us when booking or visiting our property in Kodaikanal.

2. Information Collection

We collect information necessary to facilitate your stay and comply with local regulations. This includes:

  • Guest names and contact details (phone number, email address).
  • Government-mandated identification for check-in purposes (Aadhar, Passport, etc.).
  • Booking preferences and special requests to personalize your stay experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for hospitality purposes:

  • Managing reservations and confirming bookings.
  • Providing travel assistance and curated hill tours via our travel desk.
  • Ensuring safety and security during your stay.
  • Fulfilling legal obligations, such as sharing guest details with local authorities via Form C.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party marketers.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to ensure your personal details are stored safely. Payment information is processed through secure, encrypted gateways and is not stored on our local servers.

5. Cookies and Web Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of certain site features.
