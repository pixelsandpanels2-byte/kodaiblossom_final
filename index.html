Welcome to Kodai Blossom

Cottage Stay in Kodaikanal

Kodai Blossom is a peaceful cottage homestay in Kodaikanal, located just 2 km from Kodaikanal Lake. Set in a quiet area, it offers easy access to the town while ensuring a calm and relaxing stay.

Ideal for families and group travelers, our homestay features spacious rooms, common living areas, and a garden space to unwind. With essential amenities and a warm, home-like atmosphere, it’s a perfect choice for a comfortable and budget-friendly stay in Kodaikanal.
